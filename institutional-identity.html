Quick Understanding
Simple logic behind this module.
Vision and Mission should come first
This module should be completed before moving into deeper planning because all later modules should align with it.
Vision = Final Direction
Vision explains what the school wants to become or create over time.
Mission = Way of Working
Mission explains how the school will work daily and strategically to move toward that vision.
